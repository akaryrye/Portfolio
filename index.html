About Me

My whole life, I have been fascinated with computers and everything internet. I began my web-development journey way back in High School, when the internet was brand new. I learned to do quite a bit with HTML ... but that is really all there was back then. Life took me in another direction over the next several years, but I still spent much time on computers, playing games (WOW anyone?), building systems, troubleshooting for family and friends, and setting up local networking solutions.

Fast forward to 2019, I became interested in pursuing a career in web development, so I started learning everything I could on my own. I took Harvard's CS50 online, did the Freecodecamp curriculum, as well as much searching on google and stackoverflow. I tackled many small projects along the way, but I felt like I was starting to hit a wall on my own. The decision to join a bootcamp came with some reservation, due to cost, and uncertainty as to weather anyone would take me seriously in the end. What made me ultimately decide to do so was that I had no other realistic options, and I knew that working with other people would help me to level up.

I am excited to learn and eager to improve my skills, and eventually do so as a career! Please check out my projects, and if you would like to get in touch (I wont bite, hehe), the contact page has everything you need.
